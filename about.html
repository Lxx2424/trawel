Khazar Travel Story

Khazar Travel is one of the leading travel and tourism companies in Baku, Azerbaijan. It has been operating since 1999. There are three offices located in the city centre and 50 people working under Khazar brand.

Khazar Travel has an extensive network with-in the country and in neighbouring countries such as Georgia, Turkey and Russia. The company is proud to collaborate with hand-picked professional partners worldwide. With experience in depth, we offer industry partners the full benefits of a customized 360-degree service catering to a full range of client needs and delivering the best possible experiences.

Our team are dedicated to meet any require- ment with the most flexibility for any custom designed package regardless the size of the group (from small special interest groups to extended congresses) in order to meet any group specification and budget restriction.

We emphasize creativity, cost-effciency and excellence in designing, planning and implementing outstanding group travel, corporate meetings, incentives and special events. We have rich experience with international tourism market and our services are not limited to only meeting and accommodating our guests.

Throughout the years we have built up a vast network of reliable local suppliers and associates with whom we have strong strategic partnerships. We work closely with more than 300 hotels, all types of dining venues, activity providers, venues, licensed guides, best hotel prices etc.

Khazar Travel is a leading sale representative for an airline in the country. We provide these airlines with a dedicated team of qualified people in Baku with airline and sales experience to grow and promote partners' services to local travel agents, corporates and the general public.

Our Mission

To offer competitive prices and high-level services to achieve total client satisfaction. We want to meet and exceed all business client goals and objectives, strive for excellence in quality, integrity, and value in all that we do.

Our Vision

To strengthen our position as the leading tourism company providing quality creative, innovative, competitive and socially responsible services in the region. We take care of each of our clients in all aspects; security, the economy, a good impres impression about the trip.
